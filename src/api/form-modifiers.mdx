## API: Form modifiers

> How to update the state of your form


- `setPristine(pristine: boolean): boolean`
- `setDirty(dirty: boolean): boolean`
- `setValid(valid: boolean): boolean`
- `setEnabled(enabled: boolean): boolean`
- `setTouched(touched: boolean): boolean`
- `setSaved(saved: boolean): boolean`
- `setData(data: any)`
